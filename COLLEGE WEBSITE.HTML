Admission Process

Admissions into B.Tech programs are based on AP EAPCET ranks. 70% seats through Category A, 30% through Management Quota.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry & Maths.
  • Minimum marks as per APSCHE norms.
  • Management seats require Category B counselling.
